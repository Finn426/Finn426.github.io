About Grooming

Here at Furry friends we offer a great service for grooming your pet. We can do hair trimming, flea removal, disinfection, deep cleaning, and warm baths. We gaurantee that your pet will come back to you squeaky clean! Our special cleaning formula is unlike any other and ensures that your dog will smell great for a while.

Booking information

To organise grooming for your pooch, navigate to the Contact page where you can fill out the form with the necessary information required. The maximum time a dog can stay at the hotel at one time is 30 days.
