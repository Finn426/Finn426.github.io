About doggy hotel

Here at Furry Friends we can give your pet access to the best doggy hotel around! with heating all night long your dog will always be comfy and cosy while they stay! Each dog gets their own bed segregated from the other dogs so that they can get a nice peaceful sleep. Upon your dogs arrival at the doggy hotel they will recieve a toy and treat free of charge!

Booking information

To organise a stay at the doggy hotel, navigate to the Contact page where you can fill out the form with the necessary information required. The maximum time a dog can stay at the hotel at one time is 30 days.
